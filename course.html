Our Courses

Courses We Offer

We offer a diverse range of undergraduate (BTech) and postgraduate (MTech) courses, catering to the evolving needs of the engineering industry. With a blend of theoretical knowledge and practical application, these programs empower students to excel in their chosen fields and become industry leaders.

Undergraduation

We offer a dynamic range of undergraduate programs including Computer Science, specialized in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AIML) and Data Science, along with Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE), Information Technology (IT), Electronics and Instrumentation Engineering (EIE), and Mechanical Engineering (MECH). Prepare to excel in diverse engineering realms with us.

Postgraduation

CVR College of Engineering provides advanced postgraduate (MTech) programs designed to hone engineering expertise and drive innovation. With specialized tracks in fields such as Computer Science, Electronics and Communication Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering, our MTech offerings empower students to deepen their knowledge and become leaders in their chosen disciplines.

Our Facilities

Our facilities include cutting-edge laboratories, modern classrooms, extensive libraries, and recreational areas designed to enhance both learning and leisure.

World Class Library

Our world-class library offers an extensive collection of books, journals, and digital resources, providing an ideal environment for research and study. Equipped with modern amenities and a serene atmosphere, it's a hub for knowledge and academic excellence.

Playarea

Our vibrant play area offers a perfect blend of recreation and relaxation for students. With well-maintained sports facilities and green spaces, it's an ideal place to unwind, stay active, and foster a sense of camaraderie among peers.

Tasty and Healthy Food

Indulge your taste buds at our inviting cafeteria, where delicious meals, snacks, and beverages await. With a cozy ambiance and diverse menu options, it's the perfect spot to refuel and socialize between classes.!
